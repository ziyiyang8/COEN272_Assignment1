   <section class="section inqueries"> 
    <div class="section-content"> 
     <div class="row small-offset-1"> 
      <h3 class="small-10">Sales and Product&nbsp;Inquiries</h3> 
      <div class="column large-5 small-10"> 
       <h4 class="first">Apple Online Store</h4> 
       <p><a href="/">Apple.com</a> is a convenient place to purchase Apple products and accessories from Apple and other manufacturers. You can buy online, <a href="/us/shop/goto/chat/start?pg=COM:contact&amp;ap=COM" id="cta-chatlink">chat</a>, or call <span class="nowrap">(800) MY–APPLE</span> <span class="nowrap">(800–692–7753)</span>, 7&nbsp;days a week from 4:00 a.m. to 10:00 p.m. Pacific time.</p> 
       <p>You can get information about an order you placed on the Apple Online Store through the <a href="/orderstatus/">Order Status</a> page. If you prefer, you can also get order status or make changes by phone at <span class="nowrap">(800) 692–7753</span>, 7 days a week from 4:00 a.m. to 10:00 p.m. Pacific time.</p> 
       <h4>Apple Retail Stores</h4> 
       <p>Experience the digital lifestyle at any of the <a href="/retail/">Apple Retail Stores</a> around the country. Find store hours and contact information for all locations.</p> 
       <h4>Get Financing for You, Your Business, or Your School</h4> 
       <p><a href="/financing/">Apple Financial Services</a> offers financing on Apple products for consumers, educational institutions, and businesses. Speak with your Apple representative to learn more.</p> 
       <h4>Find Consultants</h4> 
       <p>Visit our <a href="http://consultants.apple.com">Apple Consultants Network</a> page to find a consultant in the U.S. or Canada.</p> 
       <h4>Find Authorized Training&nbsp;Centers</h4> 
       <p>Use our <a href="http://training.apple.com/locations">Training Center Locator</a> to find Apple Authorized Training Centers worldwide.</p> 
      </div> 
      <div class="column large-5 large-offset-1 small-10 small-offset-0"> 
       <h4 class="first">How to Buy for Business</h4> 
       <p>If you are a business or professional user, visit the <a href="/us_smb_78313/shop/goto/home">Apple Store for Business</a> or call <span class="nowrap">1–800–854–3680</span>, 7 days a week from 4:00 a.m. to 10:00 p.m. Pacific time.</p> 
       <p><strong>Corporate and Government Sales:</strong></p> 
       <ul> 
        <li>Apple Enterprise Sales <span class="nowrap">(877) 412–7753</span></li> 
        <li>Apple Government Sales <span class="nowrap">(877) 412–7753</span></li> 
       </ul> 
       <h4>How to Buy for Education</h4> 
       <p>If you are a student or teacher, visit the <a href="/us/shop/goto/educationrouting/">Apple Store for Education</a> or call 1–800–692–7753, 7 days a week from 4:00 a.m. to 10:00 p.m. Pacific&nbsp;time.</p> 
       <p>If you are buying on behalf of an educational institution, visit the <a href="https://ecommerce.apple.com/asb2b/fys.do?language=EN&amp;country=US">Apple Store for Education Institutions</a> or call <span class="nowrap">1–800–800–2775</span>, 7 days a week from 5:30 a.m. to 4:30 p.m. Pacific&nbsp;time.</p> 
       <h4>Find Apple Authorized Resellers</h4> 
       <p>Use our <a href="/buy/locator/">Reseller Locator</a> to find an Apple Authorized Reseller in the U.S.</p> 
       <p><a href="/buy/">Apple Authorized Resellers</a> offer industry expertise, multi-platform services, and Mac-based solutions for a wide variety of organizations.</p> 
       <h4>Become a Consultant, Reseller, or Service Provider</h4> 
       <p><a href="http://salesresources.apple.com/cp/channel/">Join Apple</a> as a consultant, reseller, or service provider.</p> 
      </div>

     <div class="row small-offset-1"> 
      <h3>Product and Technical&nbsp;Support</h3> 
      <div class="column large-5 small-10"> 
       <h4 class="first">Contact Apple Support</h4> 
       <p>Need service or support? <a href="https://expresslane.apple.com/">Start your request online</a> and we’ll find you a solution.</p> 
       <p>More Ways to Get Help:</p> 
       <ul class="help-list"> 
        <li><a href="/support/itunes/contact/">Contact iTunes Store support</a></li> 
        <li>U.S. iPod, Mac and iPad technical support: <span class="nowrap">(800) APL–CARE (800–275–2273)</span></li> 
        <li>U.S. iPhone technical support: (800) MY–IPHONE <span class="nowrap">(800–694–7466)</span></li> 
        <li><a href="http://support.apple.com/kb/HE57">See all worldwide support telephone numbers</a></li> 
        <li><a href="http://support.apple.com/kb/HT1937">Contact a mobile carrier</a></li> 
        <li><a href="/retail/geniusbar/">Make an appointment at an Apple Retail Store Genius&nbsp;Bar</a></li> 
        <li><a href="https://locate.apple.com/">Find an Apple Authorized Service Provider</a></li> 
        <li>Beats support: <br><span class="nowrap">(800) 442–4000 (U.S.)</span> or <a href="https://www.beatsbydre.com/support/international-numbers.html">see all worldwide support telephone numbers</a></li> 
       </ul> 
       <p class="first">If you have a hearing or vision impairment, call (877) 204–3930 for direct access to Apple representatives who are trained in providing support using accessibility&nbsp;services.</p> 
       <p class="alt">Mac, iPhone, iPod, iPad, Apple TV, and Apple display customers within 90 days of ownership are eligible for complimentary telephone technical support. <a href="/support/">Online technical support</a> for Apple products is available beyond the initial 90 days.</p> 
      </div> 
      <div class="column large-5 large-offset-1 small-10 small-offset-0"> 
       <h4 class="nowrap first">Browse Online Support</h4> 
       <p>Visit the Apple Support site for quick answers, manuals, and in-depth technical articles. Visit Apple Support Communities to get help and tips from fellow Apple&nbsp;customers.</p> 
       <p>For help with Beats by Dre headphones and speakers, visit Beats Support. For help with the Beats Music streaming service, visit Beats Music Support.</p> 
       <h4>Lost or Stolen Apple Products</h4> 
       <p>If you have lost or found an Apple product, <a href="http://support.apple.com/kb/HT2526">contact your local law-enforcement agency to report it</a>. You can also find a list of serial numbers associated with your Apple ID and get information about using Find My iPhone for iPhone, iPad, iPod touch, or&nbsp;Mac.</p> 
       <h4>Legal</h4> 
       <p>For legal questions, please go to <a href="/legal/contact/">apple.com/legal/contact</a> and select from the drop-down menu provided. To report suspected counterfeit or knockoff products, or other forms of suspected infringement of Apple intellectual property, select Counterfeits &amp; Knockoffs from the drop-down menu.</p> 
      </div>
